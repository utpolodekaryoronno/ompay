Types of Online Payment Services

  • QR Payment

    OMPAY introduces a fast and convenient way to conduct transactions. Users can simply scan QR codes to make payments, eliminating the need for physical currency or card swiping. Whether it’s in-store purchases, bill payments, or peer-to-peer transfers, QR Payment ensures a swift and efficient process, enhancing the overall convenience of financial transactions.

  • E-Wallet

    The E-Wallet streamlines online payments, eliminating the need for users to repeatedly input their payment details during transactions. It’s a widely recognized payment option across various e-commerce platforms, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free purchasing experience.

  • Auto Debit

    OMPAY’s Auto Debit solution simplifies recurring payments for both businesses and their customers. It enables businesses to establish automatic deductions from their customers’ bank accounts or credit cards, ensuring timely payments for subscription services, utility bills, and other recurring expenses.

  • Credit & Debit Card

    OMPAY allows customers to access a wide array of financial functionalities via Credit & Debit Card services. Our cards are designed for flexibility, offering a secure means to make purchases online or in-person. OMPAY’s Credit & Debit Cards are equipped with the latest security features, providing peace of mind to users while ensuring the confidentiality of their financial information.

  • Crypto Payment

    OMPAY enthusiastically embraces the dynamic realm of cryptocurrencies, extending a cutting-edge Crypto Payment solution. This offering empowers customers to utilize digital currencies to complete transactions for products and services. Our crypto payment service harnesses the capabilities of blockchain technology, ensuring transactions that are not only secure but also borderless and remarkably efficient.

  • Online Banking & Bank Transfer

    OMPAY provides a secure and customer-centric online banking payment service, facilitating direct payments from customers’ bank accounts. Our online banking platform seamlessly interfaces with a wide network of financial institutions, enabling safe and direct fund transfers.
